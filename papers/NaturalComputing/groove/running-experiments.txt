Running example experiments
===========================

- Call the Generator with
  java -Xmx12G -XX:SoftRefLRUPolicyMSPerMB=10 -jar \Path\To\Groove\bin\Generator.jar

- Call the ModelChecker with
  java -Xmx12G -XX:SoftRefLRUPolicyMSPerMB=10 -cp \Path\To\Groove\bin\Generator.jar nl.utwente.groove.explore.ModelChecker 


Producing the full state space
------------------------------
- DFS without forbidden detection:
  -P running.properties -D "ruleEnabling+=-forbidden +present" .\rs-explore.gps

Producing the occurrence graph
------------------------------
- Producing the forbidden trace as control program
  -P .\running.properties -D "ruleEnabling=+fired" -s bfs -a formula:forbidden -r 1 -traces -o running.gcp .\rs-explore.gps\

- Copy the control program to the build system
  cp .\running.gcp .\rs-build.gps\

- DFS without forbidden detection:
  -P cmsb.properties -D "disabledRules+=fired present forbidden" .\rs-explore.gps
  
- BFS without forbidden detection:
  -P cmsb.properties -D "disabledRules+=fired present forbidden" -s bfs .\rs-explore.gps
  
- DFS with major forbidden detection:
  -P cmsb.properties -D "disabledRules+=fired present" -D "typeGraph+=cmsb.major-forbidden" -s uptorule:dfs->forbidden -a formula:forbidden .\rs-explore.gps
  
- DFS with moderate forbidden detection:
  -P cmsb.properties -D "disabledRules+=fired present" -D "typeGraph+=cmsb.moderate-forbidden" -s uptorule:dfs->forbidden -a formula:forbidden .\rs-explore.gps
  
- DFS with minor forbidden detection:
  -P cmsb.properties -D "disabledRules+=fired present" -D "typeGraph+=cmsb.minor-forbidden" -s uptorule:dfs->forbidden -a formula:forbidden .\rs-explore.gps
  
- DFS with major+moderate forbidden detection:
  -P cmsb.properties -D "disabledRules=fired present forbidden maude.markInput maude.start maude.steady" -s uptorule:dfs->cmsb.major-and-moderate -a formula:cmsb.major-and-moderate .\rs-explore.gps


Bounded DFS generation
----------------------
- Up to step n, major risks:
  -P cmsb.properties -D "typeGraph+=cmsb.major-forbidden" -D "disabledRules+=fired present" -s uptorule:dfs2n->forbidden -a formula:forbidden .\rs-explore.gps


Model checking
--------------
- Generation of the model: call the Generator with
  -P cmsb.properties -D "disabledRules+=fired" -D "controlProgram=atomic" -o cmsb.aut .\rs-explore.gps

- Model checking: call the ModelChecker with
  -ctl property cmsb.aut
  where property is

-ctl "AX(afib           & heart_rate & consensus_acei & !over75                       & !doac_int & hyper -> EF forbidden)" `
-ctl "AX(afib           & heart_rate & consensus_acei & !over75            & diabetes & !doac_int         -> EF forbidden)" `
-ctl "AX(afib           & heart_rate & consensus_acei &  over75 & !below55            & !doac_int         -> EF forbidden)" `
-ctl "AX(afib & has_fib              & consensus_acei                                 & !doac_int & hyper -> EF forbidden)" `
-ctl "AX(afib & has_fib                                                                           & hyper -> EF forbidden)" `
-ctl "AX(afib & has_fib & heart_rate                                                                      -> EF forbidden)" `

-ctl "AX((afib           & heart_rate & consensus_acei & !over75                       & !doac_int & hyper | `
          afib           & heart_rate & consensus_acei & !over75            & diabetes & !doac_int         | `
          afib           & heart_rate & consensus_acei &  over75 & !below55            & !doac_int         | `
          afib & has_fib              & consensus_acei                                 & !doac_int & hyper | `
          afib & has_fib                                                                           & hyper | `
          afib & has_fib & heart_rate                                                                      ) <-> EF forbidden)"
